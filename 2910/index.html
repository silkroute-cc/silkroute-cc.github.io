倾听马来西亚 | 新旧之间——吉隆坡，全球线上首播

寸土黄金的首都，发展与变迁已是它不变的宿命？

曾经，它只是一个游子城，但现在，它是我们孩子的故乡。

当新地标不断涌现，对这座城市的认知逐渐变得片面时，我们还能够做些什么，来留住这座城160多年的历史记忆？

新生代喜欢在旧情怀中寻找新鲜感，但这股新潮流是否需要更扎实的文化底蕴来撑起？

新与旧之间，平衡点在哪里？

The Voice of Malaysia - Between the New and Old: Kuala Lumpur

The capital Kuala Lumpur, is development and change becoming the city’s destiny?

Once it was just a city filled with people from other town, but now it is the hometown of our children. People’s perception of the city is becoming more partial as new landmarks emerge. What else can we do to preserve the memory of this 160-year-old city?

The new generation likes to find freshness in old sentiments, but does this new trend need a more solid cultural heritage to hold it up?

Where is the balance between the new and the old?、
