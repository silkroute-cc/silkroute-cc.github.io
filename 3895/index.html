2023大熊猫全球生日会（马来西亚站）绘画及生日寄语征集

大熊猫是马来西亚民众的宠儿！8月23日，中国旅马大熊猫“兴兴”和“靓靓”即将迎来17岁的生日！

为了让全民参与大熊猫生日趴，吉隆坡中国文化中心携手马来西亚国家动物园诚邀您参与大熊猫生日趴的创意活动——“天府四川·熊猫家园”之大熊猫生日趴（马来西亚站）绘画及生日寄语征集活动。

赶快挥动画笔，并在作品中留下对大熊猫的生日祝福，表达您对大熊猫的热爱和美好期许，同时感受来自四川的生态自然和烟火人间之美！

所有入选作品将有机会于国家动物园熊猫馆展出！

活动详情请浏览官网：

https://www.chinaculturalcentre.my/cn-giantpandadrawingandbirthdaymessagecontest2023/

询问：

电话：603-21812456/ WhatsApp 019-6271048 (邱先生)

电邮：project@chinaculturalcentre.my

报名截止日期：9/8/2023 （星期三），下午3时

Attention all panda enthusiasts in Malaysia! Join us in celebrating the 17th birthday of our beloved pandas, "Xing Xing" and "Liang Liang," on August 23rd!

Organized by China Cultural Centre in Kuala Lumpur and in collaboration with the Zoo Negara Malaysia invites you to participate in a creative activity - "Giant Panda Birthday Celebration Party" Malaysia Drawing and Birthday Message Contest. Express your love and hopeful wishes for the Giant Panda through your artwork, while immersing yourself in the cultural richness of Sichuan.

Grab your paintbrush and make this birthday celebration unforgettable!

The shortlisted artworks will stand a chance to exhibit at Zoo Negara Malaysia!

For more details, please visit our official website:
