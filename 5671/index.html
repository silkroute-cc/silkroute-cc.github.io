Artful Year-End | Final Term of 2025 Art Courses Now Open for Registration!

In Malaysia’s warm September, the days remain bright and vibrant, perfect for dedicating time to art and culture.

From Basic Claborate-style Painting, Calligraphy, Guzheng, and Weiqi to Chinese Ink Painting, let art enrich your everyday life and carry forward the beauty of tradition.
