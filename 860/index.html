马来西亚豫商联合会简介

马来西亚豫商联合会（MALAYSIA HENAN ENTREPRENEURSHIP ASSOCIATION，以下简称MHEA）于2019年在吉隆坡成立，是由在马豫籍企业、豫籍经济贸易届人士和团体依法注册成立的、代表会员利益、维护会员权益、为所有会员服务的非盈利、非宗教、非政治性质的民间社团组织。

MHEA的宗旨是”诚实、守信、爱心、奉献”，为在马来西亚的豫商会员提供服务，维护会员正当权益，建立商务平台，沟通信息，交流经验，发挥桥粱和纽带作用；促进马来西亚与中国河南省及其它地区之间的贸易、投资和经济技术合作，推动双方文化交流，增进中马两国人民及经济贸易界的相互了解与友谊。

MHEA的成立受到了中国河南省贸促会、河南省豫商联合会的大力支持和鼓励，同时也与马来西亚本地的商会，律师、会计师等专业协会组织建立了友好合作、守望相助的良好关系。目前拥有**家会员企业，覆盖了金融、信息科技、建筑、电讯、能源、零售、制造、教育、医疗、传媒、餐饮、旅游等行业，是马来西亚极为活跃的团体组织。

MHEA通过对会员企业的走访，组织会员集体活动，参加国内、国际举办的各类讲座论坛等多种形式，大力促进马豫双方文化交流，宣传豫籍企业，介绍河南及马来西亚优势物产及旅游资源，解决会员在马来西亚遇到的法律、语言、工作、学习等各种问题，受到了广大会员及中马双方政府的认可。

疫情当下，诸业维艰，MHEA在此艰难时刻，仍将铭记使命，坚定前行。

让温暖永不缺席！

Introduction

MALAYSIA HENAN ENTREPRENEURSHIP ASSOCIATION (henceforth referred to as MHEA) is established in Kuala Lumpur of Malaysia in 2019.And it is registered and established legally by HeNan person who established enterprises and organizations in Malaysia.MHEA is a non-profit, non-religious, non-political civil society organization that represents and protects the interests of its members and serves all members.

The tenet of MHEA is ‘honesty, trustworthiness, love, dedication’, and serving HeNan merchant members who are in Malaysia,thus, MHEA maintains the legitimate rights and interests of members, establishes business platform, communicates information, exchanges experience, and plays the role of bridge and link.Otherwise,MHEA also promotes trade between Malaysia and Henan Province of China and other regions, enhances investment and economic and technical cooperation, improves bilateral cultural exchanges, enhances mutual understanding and friendship between the people of China and Malaysia.

The establishment of MHEA has been strongly supported and encouraged by China Council for the promotion of International Trade Henan sub-Council and HeNan Entrepreneurship Association.Meanwhile, it also has established a friendly, cooperative and helpful relationship with local chambers, lawyers, accountants and other professional associations in Malaysia.Currently, MHEA has *** member enterprises, covering various industries such as finance, information technology, construction, telecommunications, energy, retailing, manufacturing, education, medical treatment, media, catering and tourism, etc. Now, MHEA is a quite active organization in Malaysia.

MHEA visits its member enterprises, and organize collective activities for members, hence, participates in various lectures and forums held at home and abroad, vigorously, promotes the cultural exchanges between Malaysia and HeNan province, publicizes Henan enterprises, introduces superior products and tourism resources in Henan and Malaysia, solves various problems encountered by members in Malaysia such as law, language, work and study. Finally,it has been recognized by the majority of members and the governments of China and Malaysia.

At the moment of the epidemic, all industries are struggling.In this difficult time, MHEA will still remember its mission and move forward firmly.

Let the warmth never be absent!
