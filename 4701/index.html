活动预告 | “享自然之趣·品茶和天下”——文冬雅集

中国是茶的故乡，“人在草木间”是为“茶”。

5月18日，“茶和天下·雅集”品牌活动再度回归。今年我们将带领大家走入远离尘嚣的山野，在风景如画的文冬休闲农场（Bentong Farm），与自然为伴，品茶聆曲，还可以和动物交朋友，体验别开生面的茶中“动物园”，让你在大自然的交响中感受中国茶文化三千年来“日用而不觉”的美。

本次活动由吉隆坡中国文化中心和中国对外文化交流协会共同主办，文冬休闲农场和喜茶（深圳）企业管理有限责任公司为合作单位，并得到中马社会各界的关心和支持。

位于彭亨州文冬暹猛原住民区的“文东休闲农场”，距首都吉隆坡约1小时车程，占地27英亩，是由一位在城市长大，喜欢与动物打交道，并响往回归大自然生活的孩子在乡区所打造的“乌托邦”。农场背山望野，不仅环境优美，小桥流水，还拥有超过 80 种当地果树和蔬菜。园内的宠物园饲养超过 100种世界各地不同的稀有动物与宠物，如有 “白雪公主” 之称的白化缅甸蟒、 “偷心贼” 的浣熊、动物界的 “钓鱼翁” 鹈鹕、色彩缤纷的鹦鹉、狮子王里的丁满狐蒙、粉红水牛等，还有精彩的越野摩托等户外活动等您前来体验。

千万别错过这场寻找自然之美、品味茶香之旅的机会！5月18日（星期六），让我们相聚于The Bentong Farm文冬休闲农场，共同探寻自然的美，品味茶香之旅！

活动详情

EventAnnouncement | Tea for Harmony · Yaji Cultural Salon 2024 @ #Bentong

Co-organized by the China lnternational Culture Association and China Cultural Centre in Kuala Lumpur, supported by Heytea MY and The Bentong Farm, the "Tea for Harmony Yaji Cultural Salon 2024 (Bentong, Malaysia)” will immerse you in the world of tea, offering a unique experience of harmony between tea and nature, along with the opportunity to interact closely with rare animals, creating a magical world that combines a tea house with a zoo.

The Bentong Farm located just 1-hour-drive from Kuala Lumpur. The Bentong Farm lives up to its expectations as an Eden with its magnificent display of nature and beautiful landscapes where one can escape from the city bustle to peace and tranquillity. Encompassing 27 acres of land with its background of mountains and serene landscape, the farm boasts over 80 varieties of local fruits and vegetables. Its petting zoo is home to over 100 rare animals and pets from around the world, including the "Snow White" Python Bivittatus, the "Heart-stealer" Raccoon, the "Fishing Grandpa" Pelican, Vibrant Parrots, characters reminiscent of "The Lion King," and even Pink Water Buffaloes. Exciting outdoor activities such as ATV rides provide an adrenaline-pumping experience, allowing visitor to traverse off-road trails, encounter stunning natural wonders, and immerse themselves in the beauty of the surroundings.

Don't miss out on this opportunity to explore the beauty of nature and savor the aroma of tea! Join us on May 18th (Saturday) at The Bentong Farm for an unforgettable journey of discovering nature's beauty and indulging in the world of tea!、
