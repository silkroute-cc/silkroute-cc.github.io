【最新活动】龙年过节Chinese New Year Gathering2024

2024年新春将至，中国马来西亚商会再次举办新年聚会，邀请各界马来西亚同胞、会员及朋友们一起庆祝，祈愿今年风生水起，合家欢乐，万事如意！

As we welcome the new year of 2024, MayCham once again organizes the Chinese New Year gathering, inviting all Malaysians, Members and Friends to celebrate the coming Year of The Dragon!
