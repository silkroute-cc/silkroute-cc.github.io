何以中国，必至宁夏。

宁夏，是大自然赐给祖国西北的一块瑰宝。

黄河，将母亲最温柔的臂弯留给了宁夏，它在这里安宁、平缓，触手可及。

贺兰山，是宁夏人民的父亲山，它阻挡了腾格里沙漠的侵蚀，呈现了塞上江南被沙漠环绕的地理奇观。

宁夏南部的六盘山，是中国工农红军翻越的最后一座大山，红军长征在这里胜利会师，胜利之山，永放光芒。

宁夏被誉为“葡萄酒之都”，贺兰山东麓是世界上最适合种植酿酒葡萄和生产高端葡萄酒的黄金地带之一，昔日“干沙滩”，今日“金沙滩”，贺兰山东麓葡萄酒假以时日“当惊世界殊”。

宁夏被誉为“中国滩羊之乡”，宁夏的滩羊肉不仅不膻还很嫩，可以让不吃羊肉的人愿意在宁夏“破戒”。

宁夏被誉为“星星的故乡”，六盘山上，贺兰山下，长城内外，黄河两岸，随处抬头都能夜观满天的星河，如诗如画，如梦似幻。

行走宁夏，就是要为您开启新的旅程，在大西北的空间里，提供一种全新的生活方式，在宁夏，夏天享受的是清凉，冬季享受的是阳光，一年四季享受的是轻松安宁的生态环境，文旅相伴，让生活更美好。目前，宁夏正在努力建设黄河流域生态保护和高质量发展先行区，打造西部国际旅游目的地，焕发出新的魅力。

祖国那么大，一起来宁夏，品红酒、吃羊肉、观星空，给心灵放个假。让您静静心、歇歇脚、再出发！

Ningxia is a must-see for whoever that travels to China.

Ningxia is a treasure land bestowed by Nature to China in its northwest.

Close within reach, the Yellow River flows peacefully through Ningxia, gently embracing it in her arms.

The father-like Helan Mountain is like a shield blocking the advance of the Tengger Desert for the Ningxia people, presenting a geographical spectacle surrounded by desert a geographical spectacle resembling that of Jiangnan watertown in the hinterland of China.

The Liupan Mountain in the south of Ningxia was the last mountain the Red Army climbed over in the Long March. Known as the "Mountain of Triumph", it is an important military retreat undertaken by the Red Army to evade the pursuit of the Kuomintang army, hence its importance in China’s revolutionary history.

Ningxia is known as the "wine capital", and the eastern foothills of the Helan Mountain represent one of the golden areas for growing wine grapes and producing high-end wines in the world. It used to be a "barren desert", but today it has transformed into a "golden land". The wines at the eastern foothills of Helan Mountain will "amaze the world" over time.

Ningxia is the "Hometown of Tan Sheep in China". The Tan Sheep meat in Ningxia is tender but not gamy, so that even those not used to eating mutton would “break their rules”.

Ningxia is also known as the "Dreamland of the Stars". At the top of Liupan Mountain, or at the foot of Helan Mountain, visitors may enjoy the great scenery of the Great Wall. On both sides of the Yellow River, visitors will see the star-lit sky whenever they look up at night, as if in a wonderland.

Traveling in Ningxia is to start a new journey and a new way of life in the vast land of China’s northwest. In Ningxia, you can enjoy a cool summer, a winter full of sunshine, and a relaxing and peaceful ecological environment all year round. It will be a traveler’s life enriched by culture. At present, Ningxia is striving to build a pilot area for ecological protection and high-quality development in the Yellow River Basin. It has become a charming international destination in China’s west.

In China’s vast land, come to Ningxia to taste its red wine and mutton, watch the starry sky, and give your heart a holiday. Ningxia, a place to refresh yourself, and a place to start again!
