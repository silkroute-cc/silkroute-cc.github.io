马航关于对入境中国航班的行前闭环健康管理的通知

据马来西亚航空消息，7月28日起，马航吉隆坡-广州航线实施5天4夜行前闭环管理措施，适用于从2022年8月1日起乘坐MH376由吉隆坡国际机场飞往广州白云机场的所有乘客。

隔离酒店为：Eastin Hotel Kuala Lumpur

隔离费用为：1700令吉起

马来西亚航空公司网址：www.malaysiaairlines.com

具体要求和规定请阅读（中文）：

Entry Requirements to China

Malaysia Airlines Implements ‘Pre-Departure Health Screening Programme’ for Passengers Travelling via Flight MH376 from Kuala Lumpur (KUL) to Guangzhou (CAN)

As part of our efforts to protect and ensure passenger safety, prevent further spread of the COVID-19 virus, and ensure seamless travel experiences for our passengers, Malaysia Airlines will be implementing a dedicated ‘Pre-Departure Health Screening Programme’ applicable to all passengers on flight MH376 departing from Kuala Lumpur International Airport (KUL) to Guangzhou Baiyun International Airport (CAN) effective 1 August 2022.

The Pre-Departure Health Screening Programme is an initiative implemented by the airline as an added layer of precaution in facilitating seamless travel to China and is compulsory for all Malaysia Airlines’ passengers departing to China from Kuala Lumpur.

All passengers travelling via flight MH376 will need to undergo a 5-days/4-nights, Pre-Departure Health Screening Programme’ at the designated hotel accommodation by the airline’s service provider and COVID-19 tests as regulated by the Chinese Embassy in the host country and Malaysia Airlines.

Health Requirements and Passenger Flow

Below are the 6-steps requirement under the Pre-Departure Health Screening Programme, which will take effect as early as five (5) days before the scheduled departure to China.

To ensure smooth processing and communications, the entire process within the Programme will be facilitated by a service provider in close collaboration with the airline.

Below is a sample scenario of a passenger scheduled to depart on 1 August 2022.

Important: Passengers who are detected positive for COVID-19 within the Programme duration will not be allowed to continue their travel.

Packages Offered

The Programme package starts from MYR 1,700 for 5-days, 4-night stay per passenger. The price is inclusive of but not limited to:

  • Hotel accommodation
  • Meals (Breakfast/Lunch/Dinner)
  • COVID-19 test(s)
  • Transfer services from hotel to KLIA
  • Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) set

Dedicated Check-In Counters

Passengers travelling on flight MH376 will be escorted by the service provider and to proceed to the dedicated check-in counters located at Level 5, Departure Hall, KLIA.
