活动预告｜《少林传武者》纪录片放映交流会，开放报名

这是一个讲述少林武僧延智在马来西亚传播少林文化的故事。面对异国他乡的语言障碍、文化差异乃至生存挑战，延智师没有选择退缩，而是凭借对少林文化的深厚热爱和不屈的意志，逐渐克服困难，在马来西亚成功推广了中华武术，并赢得了当地民众的尊重与爱戴。这不仅展示了中华民族坚忍不拔的精神风貌，也彰显了文化交流与融合的巨大价值。

本片结合中马建交50周年和“中马友好年“的历史节点，通过个人奋斗和集体努力的叙事框架，展现了中华文化的独特魅力、人性中的美好以及不同文化间的和谐共融，激发观众对于坚守信念、文化多样性和国际友谊的深入思考。

6月2日（周日），下午2时，吉隆坡中国文化中心携导演刘乐（广州南方学院副教授）最新纪录片《少林传武者》以及片中主角少林武僧延智在中国文化中心多功能厅与大家零距离会面。届时，吉隆坡少林文化中心团队将亲临现场与观众互动交流，并展示少林功夫！除了纪录片放映，现场还准备了系列活动！敬请期待！免费入场，坐满即止！报名趁早！

Announcement Film Screening |《Shaolin Martial Artist》Documentary Film Screening & Interactive Session is open for register NOW!

This film tells the story of Shaolin monk Yan Zhi spreading Shaolin culture in Malaysia. Faced with language barriers, cultural differences, and even survival challenges in a foreign land, Shaolin monk Yan Zhi did not choose to back down. Instead, relying on his deep love for Shaolin culture and indomitable will, he gradually overcame difficulties and successfully promoted Chinese martial arts in Malaysia, winning the respect and love of the local people. This not only demonstrates the indomitable spirit of the Chinese nation, but also highlights the enormous value of cultural exchange and integration.

This film combines the historical milestone of the 50th anniversary of the establishment of friendly diplomatic relations between China and Malaysia, and through a narrative framework of individual struggle and collective efforts, showcases the unique charm of Chinese culture, the beauty of human nature, and the harmony between different cultures.

We’re proud to present you the latest documentary film "Shaolin Martial Artist" which directed by Liu Le, the Associate Professor of Nafang College GuangZhou on 2nd June (Sunday), 2PM at China Cultural Centre in Kuala Lumpur together with the protagonist, Shaolin monk Yan Zhi.

Following the screening, the director and the team from the Shaolin Cultural Center in Kuala Lumpur will have an interaction with the audience, and showcasing Shaolin Kung Fu on the spot! Admission is FREE, seating is limited and available on a first-come, first-served basis. Register NOW! More relevant activities coming soon! Stay tuned!
