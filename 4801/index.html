浓情端午·中医讲座与义诊，开放报名

又是一年的端午佳节！

作为吉隆坡中国文化中心中国节日品牌项目，2024年“浓情端午”系列活动将配合首届“你好！中国”旅游展于6月16日（周日）上午10时在武吉加里尔柏威年广场会展中心5楼（Pavilion Exhibition Centre Bukit Jalil, 5th Floor) 与大家共庆端午吃粽子！

本次“浓情端午”系列活动将以中医讲座与义诊活动为主题，由吴添发中医治疗保健中心与扶阳针道中医馆的医师们联诀带来把脉问诊、内科、针灸、推拿、拔罐和扶阳等免费义诊服务。

此外，吴添发中医医学博士教授/荣誉院士亲临现场带来主题为“脊柱健康保护与旅游健康小妙招”的健康讲座，让大家提高对自身健康的重视，同时也分享一些旅游时实用的健康小妙招。免费出席，欢迎民众报名参与。

配合端午佳节，现场还有端午图片展、限量粽子派发（仅限报名及出席讲座者）、拍照打卡领香包以及有奖问答等等精彩活动！限量礼品！座位有限，马上报名！需符合条件！

【活动详情】

日期：16/6/2024 （星期日）

时间：上午10时-下午2时

地点：武吉加里尔柏威年广场展会中心（5楼）主舞台

浓情端午·中医健康讲座

讲座主题：脊柱健康保护与旅游健康小妙招

讲座时间：10AM-11AM

主讲人：吴添发中医医学博士教授/荣誉院士

免费中医义诊

义诊时间：11AM - 2PM

包括把脉问诊、内科、针灸、推拿、拔罐与扶阳等服务。

限量端午节粽子派发

首50位报名及现场出席者将可获得中心送出端午节特备粽子，先到先得，派完即止！

名额有限，免费入场！报名链接：https://forms.gle/C3ksYPH1zYsy7Thu6

Dragon Boat Festival: TCM Health Talk & Treatment Open for Registration

As a signature event of the China Cultural Centre in Kuala Lumpur, the 2024 "Dragon Boat Festival" series event will be held on Sunday, June 16, in conjunction with the inaugural "Nihao! China" Travel Fair (Malaysia) at the Pavilion Bukit Jalil Exhibition Centre.

This year's "Dragon Boat Festival" series event will feature a theme of traditional Chinese medicine talk and free TCM consultation and treatment services. Doctors from Pusat Rawatan Tradisional Cina Goh Tiam Huat and Fuyang Zhen Dao TCM Healthcare will jointly provide free medical consultation and treatment services, including pulse diagnosis, internal medicine, acupuncture, tuina (Chinese therapeutic massage), cupping therapy, and Fuyang therapy.

The organizers are honored to invite Prof. Dr. Goh Tiam Huat / Honorary Fellowship, to deliver a health lecture on "Spine Health Protection and Travel Health." This health talk aims to raise awareness about personal health and share practical health tips for traveling. Registration is free, and the public is welcome to register.

Additionally, there will be a Dragon Boat Festival photo exhibition, limited-edition rice dumpling set distribution (available only to those who register for the TCM health talk), photo booth activities with gifts, and a quiz with prizes. Stay tuned for the exciting series event!

【Event Details】

Date：16/6/2024 (Sunday)

Venue：Pavilion Exhibition Centre Bukit Jalil, Level 5, Pink Zone

【TCM Health Talk】

Theme：Spine Health Protection and Travel Health

Time：10AM-11AM

Speaker：Prof. Dr. Goh Tiam Huat / Honorary Fellowship

【FREE TCM Consultation & Treatment】 Service Time：11AM - 2PM

Including pulse diagnosis and consultation, internal medicine, acupuncture, Tuina (Chinese therapeutic massage), cupping therapy, and Fuyang therapy.

【Limited Giveaways】

Time: 12PM

The first 50 people to register for the health talk and attend the event will receive a special Dragon Boat Festival rice dumpling gift set from the CCCKL. The offer is on a first-come, first-served basis!

Registration Link: https://forms.gle/C3ksYPH1zYsy7Thu6
