隧道股份城建国际董事长王旋东调研马来西亚市场

Wang Xuandong, Secretary of the Party Committee and Chairman of SUCGI, Surveyed the Malaysian Market

调研走访境外区域中心

为国际化发展注入动能

Visit overseas regional centers

Inject kinetic energy into international development

近日，隧道股份城建国际党委书记、董事长王旋东带队考察调研马来西亚市场，城建国际副总经理马超光、副总经理陈斌、副总经理李业广、城建国际总部相关部门负责人、UTEC公司相关负责人陪同考察。

Recently, Wang Xuandong, Secretary of the Party Committee and Chairman of SUCGI, led a team to surveyed the Malaysian market, accompanied by Ma Chaoguang, Deputy General Manager of SUCGI, Chen Bin, Deputy General Manager of SUCGI, Lee Yip Kwong, Deputy General Manager of SUCGI, heads of relevant departments of SUCGI Headquarters and relevant principal of UTEC.

市场调研和项目走访

Market investigation and project visit

王旋东董事长一行听取了马来西亚市场在建项目、公司运营及市场开拓等方面内容的汇报，并与当地团队进行了交流，肯定了马来西亚市场的成立以来发展成果，同时也指出，马来西亚基建市场有自己的运作体系和模式，马来西亚工作团队要打开思路，融入当地“朋友圈”，在做好EPC主营业务的同时关注BOT及PPP优质项目机会，希望在上海总部及新加坡区域中心的支持帮助下，通过在建项目积累的良好口碑，使城建国际马来西亚新市场再上一个新台阶！

Chairman Wang Xuandong and his delegation listened to the reports on the projects under construction in the Malaysian market, the company's operation and market development, and communicated with the local team. They affirmed the development achievements of the Malaysian market since its establishment, and also pointed out that the Malaysian infrastructure market has its own operation system and mode. The Malaysian team should open its mind and integrate into the local "circle of friends". While doing well in the main business of EPC, the team should also pay attention to the opportunities of BOT and PPP high-quality projects. The delegation hopes that with the support and assistance of the Shanghai headquarters and the Singapore regional center, the new Malaysian market of SUCGI can be raised to a new level through the good reputation of the projects under construction.

同期，王旋东董事长一行来到LRT3项目实地考察项目建设情况，并慰问一线职工，他对项目团队克服疫情带来的种种不利局面、充分发挥主观能动性、高标准高质量地推进项目进度表示了充分肯定，他勉励项目团队：目前项目已进入收尾阶段，要继续抓好施工安全，把STEC一贯的安全文化贯彻到底，同时要做好方案，妥善处理好新项目等待周期内的人员安置计划，保证核心团队稳定，为新项目落地打好基础。

During the same period, Chairman Wang Xuandong and his delegation visited the LRT3 Project, surveyed its construction on the spot, and comforted the front-line staff. He highly appreciated the project team which has overcome all kinds of adverse situations caused by the epidemic, given full play to its subjective initiative, and promoted the project progress with high standards and high quality. He encouraged the project team: At present, the project has entered the closing stage. We should continue to pay attention to construction safety, and follow STEC's consistent safety culture. At the same time, we should make a good plan, properly handle the personnel placement plan in the waiting period of the new project, ensure the stability of the core team, and lay a good foundation for the implementation of the new project.

槟城市场考察

Penang market investigation

在槟城考察期间，王旋东董事长实地调研了槟城第三跨海通道的前期选址，听取了市场开拓团队对于跨海通道项目和槟城市场跟踪的相关汇报，并与团队讨论了桥、隧两种方案在可行性、经济性、功能性对于客户选择的不同侧重，他要求，马来西亚市场团队对于新市场的进入要做好充分调研，及时掌握市场信息和当地环境，研判项目发展走向趋势，对于客户的真正需求要精准把握，以诚相待，衡量自身能力是否匹配客户需求，立足于自身工程专业背景把握合作大方向。

During his visit to Penang, Chairman Wang Xuandong conducted a field survey on the preliminary site selection of the third cross-sea channel in Penang, listened to the relevant reports of the market development team on the cross-sea channel project and Penang market tracking, and discussed with the team the different focuses of customer selection of the bridge and tunnel schemes in the aspects of feasibility, economy and functionality. He requested that the Malaysian market team should do a full research on the entry of new markets, timely grasp the market information and local environment, study and judge the development trend of the projects, accurately grasp the real needs of customers, treat them with sincerity, measure whether their capabilities match the needs of customers, and grasp the general direction of cooperation based on their own professional background of engineering.

合作伙伴交流

Partner communication

在马来西亚考察期间，王旋东董事长一行还与马来西亚Gamuda集团、ECK集团、大华银行的主要负责人会面，就地铁三期项目、槟城跨海通道项目、当地对基建项目的金融配套服务等多个共同感兴趣的内容进行了业务交流，促进了解，加深了彼此的认识。

During the visit to Malaysia, Chairman Wang Xuandong and his delegation also met with the main principals of Gamuda Group, ECK Group and United Overseas Bank in Malaysia. They have conducted business exchanges on several topics of common interest, such as the Subway Phase III Project, Penang Cross-sea Channel Project, and local financial support services for infrastructure projects, to promote and deepen mutual understanding.
